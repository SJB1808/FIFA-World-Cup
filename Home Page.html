A Flashback into the 2022 FIFA World Cup

About the World Cup
The 2022 FIFA World Cup was an international football tournament
contested by the men's national teams of FIFA's member associations
and 22nd edition of the FIFA World Cup.It took place in Qatar from
20 November to 18 December 2022.

The tournament, held every four years, was first played in 1930 in
Uruguay,and has been contested by 32 teams since the 1998 event
. The tournament was contested with eight round-robin groups
followed by a knockout round for 16 teams.
The defending champions were France, who defeated Croatia 4-2 in the 2018 FIFA World Cup Final.

The majestic event took place in 7 stadiums, namely:-
  1. Lusail Stadium - 88,966 capacity
  2. Al Bayt Stadium - 68,695 capacity
  3. Khalifa International Stadium - 45,857 capacity
  4. Ahmad bin Ali Stadium - 45,032 capacity
  5. Education City Stadium - 44,667 capacity
  6. Al Thumama Stadium - 44,400 capacity
  7. Stadium 974 - 44,089 capacity
  8. Al Janoub Stadium - 44,325 capacity
