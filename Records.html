Interesting facts about the World Cup
These are some interesting facts about the World Cup records:
  • Pele, of Brazil is the only player with 3 world cups, most assists at 10 and also is the youngest player to score in a final.
  • Lionel Messi is the player with the highest world cup appearances, at 26.
  • Miroslave Klose is the player with the highest total number of goals at World Cups, at 16.
  • Kylian Mbappe, along with Englishman Geoff Hurst has the highest number of goals in a world cup final, tied at 3.
  • Cristiano Ronaldo is the only player to have scored in 5 different editions of World Cups.
  • Brazil is the team with most World Cups won, at 5.
  • Peter Shilton of England has the highest number of total clean sheets, at 10.
